Serra de Aire e Candeeiros

"The greatest riches of the Serras de Aire e Candeeiros Natural Park are hidden within its interior. This chalk massif is a paradise for speleologists who will be able to discover grottoes and ravines in its depths, with surprising rock formations that have been sculpted by water over time."

VisitPortugal, see full article here


Serra de Aire e Candeeiros is a place directed for natural turism

In Serra de Aire e Candeeiros there are several Hotels and local accommodations to choose from, I would highly recommend the Cooking and Nature - Emotional Hotel or the Quinta do Rio de Alcaide

The option to simply camp out in the nature is also available in one of the many camping sites there present. I would recommend trying the Parque de Campismo do Arrimal or the Camping Pedreiras

Serra de Aire e Candeeiros has some water courses and fluvial beaches that are breathtaking. If you choose to visit this area of Portugal do not miss the fluvial beach of Olhos de Água

Serra de Aire e Candeeiros has several marked tracks, all of them breathtaking so just walk arround and get lost in the natural beauty of this place

Parque Natural da Arrabida

"Located next to the sea, between Setúbal and the fishing town of Sesimbra, Arrábida Natural Park has incomparable natural beauty, where the blue of the sea alternates with the white tones of the chalk cliffs and the depths of the green vegetation that covers the mountain range."

VisitPortugal, see full article here


Parque Natural da Arrabida is a place directed for natural turism

In Arrabida there are several Hotels and local accommodations to choose from, I would highly recommend the Arrábida Heritage @Portugal Luxury Retreat or the Quinta Villa Arrabida

The option to simply camp out in the nature is also available in one of the many camping sites there present. I would recommend trying the Ecoparque do Outão or the Camping Forte do Cavalo

Arrabida has many beaches that are quite simply breathtaking. If you choose to visit this area of Portugal do not miss the beach of Galapinhos and the Figueirinha beach

Parque Natural da Arrabida has several marked tracks, all of them breathtaking so just walk arround and get lost in the natural beauty of this place

Lisbon

"On the right bank of the broad Tagus river estuary, the capital of Portugal graciously reclines over rolling hills. (...) Its exceptional natural light, which has long inspired writers, photographs and filmmakers, the brightly coloured buildings straddling the slopes, the striking ochre of the roofs, the tiling on so many facades and the narrow twisting alleys of the medieval districts bestow Lisbon with the peculiar atmosphere of a city perched somewhere between the European north and the Mediterranean south."

VisitPortugal, see full article here


Lisbon is a place directed for city turism

In Lisbon there are several Hotels and local accommodations to choose from, I would highly recommend the Palácio do Governador - NAU Hotels or the Memmo Principe Real - Design Hotels

Lisbon is home to some amazing typical food such as "Pasteis de Belem" and "Pataniscas de Bacalhau", don't miss it

When visiting Lisbon, besides the amazing museums and monuments the city has to offer, be sure to walk arround the Baixa-Chiado and Alfama areas and to take a ride on the famous tram number 28

Sintra

"A beautiful town at the foot of the mountain range of the same name, its unique characteristics have led UNESCO to classify it as a World heritage site. It was even necessary to create a special category for the purpose - that of "cultural landscape" - taking into account its natural riches as well as the historic buildings in the town and mountains."

VisitPortugal, see full article here


Sintra is a place directed for city turism with a touch of nature mixed

In Sintra there are several Hotels and local accommodations to choose from, I would highly recommend the Tivoli Palácio de Seteais or the Penha Longa Resort

Sintra is home to some amazing typical food such as "Travesseiros de Sintra" and "Queijadas de Sintra", don't miss it

When visiting Sitra, besides the amazing monuments the little village has, be sure to walk arround it's green parks and enjoy the view it has to offer

Tomar

"Former seat of the Order of the Knights Templar, Tomar is a city of great charm for its artistic and cultural wealth, whose greatest expression is in the Convent of Christ, one of the chief works of the Portuguese Renaissance. "

VisitPortugal, see full article here


Tomar is a place directed for city turism with a touch of nature mixed

In Tomar there are several Hotels and local accommodations to choose from, I would highly recommend the Thomar Boutique Hotel or the Templar Hotel

Tomar is home to some amazing typical food such as "Fatias de Tomar" and "Beija-me Depressa", don't miss it

When visiting Tomar, besides the amazing monuments the little city has to offer, be sure to visit Barragem do Castelo de Bode and it's fluvial beaches, and take the boat to Castelo the Almourol, both a little outside the city, but worth the trip

Nazaré

"Nazaré beach, with its mild climate and natural beauty, has one of the most ancient traditions connected to fishing in Portugal. The long sandy half-moon shaped beach, which is also the town’s sea front, is known for its grandeur and for the brightly-coloured awnings that decorate the white sandy beaches, contrasting with the blue of the water."

VisitPortugal, see full article here


Nazaré is a place directed for beach turism

In Nazaré there are several Hotels and local accommodations to choose from, I would highly recommend the Hotel Miramar Sul or the Hotel Praia

In Nazaré you will find both Vale Paraíso Ohai Nazaré and Camping Orbitur Valado, both excelent options for those who prefer to camp

Nazaré is home to some amazing typical food such as "Caldeirada Nazarena" and "Cataplana", don't miss it

Praia da Nazaré is a blue flagged beach (considered by the authorities a clean beach) and is watched by lifeguard

Sesimbra

"Lying in a sheltered bay with calm waters, Sesimbra Beach is safe and ideal for children but also high on the list for windsurfers and underwater fishermen. Its long stretch of sand ends in the small harbour at one end and Caneiro Beach at the other."

VisitPortugal, see full article here


Sesimbra is a place directed for beach turism

In Sesimbra there are several Hotels and local accommodations to choose from, I would highly recommend the Hotel do Mar or the Sesimbra Hotel & Spa

In Sesimbra you will find Camping Forte do Cavalo, also recommended to stay when visiting Arrabida

Sesimbra is home to some amazing seafood and fresh fish, don't miss it

Praia da California in Sesimbra is a blue flagged beach (considered by the authorities a clean beach) and is watched by lifeguard
