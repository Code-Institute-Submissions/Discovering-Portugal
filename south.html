Parque Natural do Sudoeste Alentejano e Costa Vicentina

"The South West Alentejo and Vicentine Coast Natural Park is the finest preserved stretch of European coastline, covering over 100 km, from Porto Covo in the Alentejo, to Burgau in the Algarve. The Park includes various unique species of animal and plant life, and is visited by many zoologists and botanists from all parts of the world."

VisitPortugal, see full article here


Parque Natural do Sudoeste Alentejano e Costa Vicentina is a place directed for natural turism

In Costa Vicentina there are several Hotels and local accommodations to choose from, I would highly recommend the Enigma - Nature & Water Hotel and the Memmo Baleeira - Design Hotels, in this case and since the coast can be quite long, you should definitely choose two accommodations on oposite corners of the coast to make it easier to visit the whole expanse

The option to simply camp out in the nature is also available in one of the many camping sites there present. I would recommend trying the Parque Campismo Porto Côvo and the Parque Campismo do Serrão, in this case and since the coast can be quite long, you should definitely choose at least two camping sites on different corners of the coast to make it easier to visit the whole expanse

Parque Natural do Sudoeste Alentejano e Costa Vicentina has several fantastic beaches. If you choose to visit this area of Portugal do not miss the Samoqueira Beach and the Furnas Beach

The Parque Natural do Sudoeste Alentejano e Costa Vicentina has several marked tracks, all of them breathtaking so just walk around and get lost in the natural beauty of this place

Parque Natural do Vale do Guadiana

"Located in the valley of the River Guadiana, winds its way through narrow riverbanks dotted by age-old watermills, or sharp ravines covered in Mediterranean undergrowth, and offers magnificent landscape views. The area of the Natural Park also includes towns that exemplify well preserved traditional architecture."

VisitPortugal, see full article here


Parque Natural do Vale do Guadiana is a place directed for natural turism

In Parque Natural do Vale do Guadiana there are several Hotels and local accommodations to choose from, I would highly recommend the Aletejo Star Hotel - São Domingos or the Quinta do Vau

Parque Natural do Vale do Guadiana in itself does not have a camping site, however the Castro Verde Camping Park is very close by and definitely worth it

Parque Natural do Vale do Guadiana has some fluvial beaches that are breathtaking. If you choose to visit this area of Portugal do not miss the Mina de Sao Domingos fluvial beach

The Parque Natural do Vale do Guadiana has several marked tracks, all of them breathtaking so just walk around and get lost in the natural beauty of this place

Évora

"Topped by an imposing cathedral, Évora is laid out over a gently sloping hill rising out of the huge Alentejo plain. It guards its historic centre with a vast outer wall and represents a valuable cultural legacy that UNESCO has classified World Heritage. The city, with its narrow streets of Moorish origin contrasting with squares where the light floods in, holds two millennia of history."

VisitPortugal, see full article here


Évora is a place directed for city turism

In Évora there are several Hotels and local accommodations to choose from, I would highly recommend the Évora Olive Hotel or the M'AR De AR Aqueduto

Évora is located in the Alentejo region that contains some amazing typical food such as "Cozido à Portuguesa à moda do Alentejo" and "Ensopado de Borrego", don't miss it

When visiting Évora, besides the amazing museums and monuments the city has to offer, be sure to visit the Praça do Giraldo de Évora and to walk around Rua Cinco de Outubro

Tavira

"Spanning both banks of the River Gilão in a complex of narrow streets and white houses, Tavira is one of the most picturesque towns in the Algarve and it’s worth taking your time to get to know it."

VisitPortugal, see full article here


Tavira is a place directed for city turism

In Tavira there are several Hotels and local accommodations to choose from, I would highly recommend the Hotel Rural Quinta do Marco - Nature & Dining or the Vila Galé Tavira

Tavira is located in the Algarve region that contains some amazing typical food such as "Doces Algarvios" and "Conquilhas à Algarvia", don't miss it

When visiting Tavira, besides the amazing monuments and sights the city has to offer, be sure to visit the Salinas and the island of Tavira

Praia de Almograve

"Part of the Sudoeste Alentejano e Costa Vicentina Natural Park, Almograve Beach is one of the best beaches along this rocky, coastline with its many inlets."

VisitPortugal, see full article here


Praia de Almograve is a place directed for beach turism

In Almograve there are several local accommodations to choose from, I would highly recommend the Casa Azul or the Almograve Beach House

Praia de Almograve in itself does not have a camping site, however the Camping Villa Park Zambujeira is very close by and definitely worth it

Praia de Almograve is a blue flagged beach (considered by the authorities a clean beach) and is watched by lifeguard

Praia da Marinha

"Backed by a tall cliff sculptured by the effects of erosion, the beauty of Marinha Beach has been ranked as among the top 100 worldwide and given its well-preserved natural environment the Beach has retained practically its original state."

VisitPortugal, see full article here


Praia da Marinha is a place directed for beach turism

Praia da Marinha in itself does not offer any accomodations, however close by are several amazing options, from which I would highly recommend Colina Luxury Villas or Vilalara Thalassa Resort

Praia da Marinha in itself does not have a camping site, however the Camping Canelas is very close by and definitely worth it

Praia da Marinha, just like Tavire, is located in the Algarve region that contains some amazing typical food such as "Doces Algarvios" and "Conquilhas à Algarvia", don't miss it

Praia da Marinha is a blue flagged beach (considered by the authorities a clean beach) and is watched by lifeguard
